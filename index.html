Welcome

Youth Spot Bar CIC is a community-led initiative based in Barking, London. We aim to uplift, support, and inspire youth through meaningful programs, creative spaces, and safe social events.

What's New

Skate Jam Fitness

Skate Jam Fitness is all about fun, fitness, and community. We're inviting people of all ages to come together for high-energy skate workouts to upbeat music. It’s more than just exercise—it’s movement, rhythm, and joy on wheels. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, you'll leave feeling energized and connected.

Dates: Saturdays from 17th of May - 28th of June 2025

Address: Park Active Age Centre, Rectory Rd, Dagenham RM10 9SA

Time: Time: 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM

Cost:Absolutely FREE!

Area We Cover

We run weekly programs and skate clubs across Barking & Dagenham community centres and schools.

Newham youth have access to wellbeing lounges and streetball playouts during school breaks.

Redbridge residents can join our digital and creative workshops year-round at partner venues.

We host annual community festivals and mobile skate parks in Havering parks and libraries.

Hackney youth can access mentoring and creative arts programs through our partners and pop-ups.
