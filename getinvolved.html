Volunteer With Us

We're always looking for passionate, community-driven people to support our work — whether you're skilled in arts, coaching, mentoring, or just want to help out at events.

Voluntering for us would mean you'll be helping on various roles.

  • Organizing and running community events
  • Mentoring and coaching young people
  • Assisting with administrative tasks
  • Helping with fundraising and outreach

    • Join us and make a difference in the lives of young people in our community.

We offer flexible volunteering opportunities that fit your schedule, whether you can commit a few hours a week or a few days a month.

We are looking for volunteers who can commit to at least 3 hours a week.

Work Experience & Placements

At Youth Spot Bar, our work experience helps develop key skills like communication, problem-solving, and teamwork. It provides hands-on industry knowledge and builds confidence in handling professional tasks. This will allow you to gain employability and enhance your CV while networking and building connections.

Roles and Responsibilities

During your time with us, there will be many roles that you can pick from, such as:

  • Skate marshal
  • Skate coach
  • Sports coordinator
  • Smoothie Bar assistant
  • Front of house
  • Maintenance Manager and lots more!

Benefits of Working with Youth Spot Bar

Being able to work with other organisations such as the Barking and Dagenham council and our collaboration partners. While being able to obtain a reference from us and the opportunity for paid work.

Incentives Working with Youth Spot Bar

  • Travel cost
  • Outings such as restaurant or going to the movies
  • Gift Vouchers etc.

Age 13-18: We also accept children 10 years and above with the consent of their parent.

We offer hands-on work experience opportunities for students, young professionals, and career changers looking to support youth development and community outreach.

Here, you will find our links to join our workshops or programmes, some of which are paid/non-paid or offer gift vouchers.

Sign up to Easyfundraising

All you need to do is sign up and shop via the easyfundraising website or App when you're shopping with over 7,500 brands including eBay, Tesco, Marks & Spencer, Just Eat and more!

How to support Youth Spot Bar C.I.C:

  1. Sign up: Provide us with a few details to set up your profile with easyfundraising.
  2. Download our browser extension: Our Donation Reminder plug-in pops up automatically when you shop to let you know when a donation is available.
  3. Track your donations: You’ll be able to see how much you’ve raised in your account and we’ll send updates on when Youth Spot Bar C.I.C gets paid.
