Roller Skate Club

Come and roll with us at skate Club. All ages are welcome, starting from 5 years old and upwards.

Skate club operates both in schools and on open days for the public.

All skates gear and roller skates are provided to get you rolling with us.

Our indoor roller skating sessions promote health, teamwork, and fun. With themed nights and open community skates, we give youth a safe, exciting space to be active and social.

Don't forget to ask about our free skate loyalty card scheme.

We also run regular afterschool roller skating sessions at:

  • Southern Road Primary School
  • Keir Hardie Primary School
  • Gascoigne Primary School

Our loyalty card scheme is designed to reward children, teens, and adults for their participation. By attending a certain number of sessions, participants can earn exciting rewards:

  • Children and Teens: A free pair of roller skates at no extra cost.
  • Adults: A £30 gift voucher to put towards purchasing their own pair of skates.

Join us to roll into fun, fitness, and rewards!

Days and Time

  • Tuesdays: 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm
  • Wednesdays: 5:00 pm - 6:30 pm
  • Fridays: 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm

Touch of Urban

We run 1-on-1 and group mentoring for personal growth, leadership skills, and career readiness. Led by trusted adults and positive role models.

At Youth Spot Bar, we deliver jewellery making workshops for those ages 8 years and upwards. Called Young Urban Creators teaching children, teens, and adults on how to make jewellery from wood, eco-friendly plastic, stones, and seashells.

We take part in markets to showcase and sell our handmade jewellery wear in fundraising to support our jewellery workshops and other community activities we do. Which candidates can participate in, if registered on our work experience programme.

Workshop Fee: £2 (learning how to make)

Workshop Time: Thursdays from 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm

To join this as part of our work experience programme taking on an Assistant / Coordinator role, see the link below. Age 13 and upwards. (Free to join)

Location: Barking Learning Centre, Youth Spot Bar Community Hub, IG11 7NB

Dance Fitness Session

Our Dance Fitness Sessions are designed to promote physical health, confidence, and creativity. Participants will enjoy a mix of dance styles, fitness routines, and fun group activities.

Hosting & Facilitating Workshops
