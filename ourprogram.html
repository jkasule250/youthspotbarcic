Skate Club

Join our Skate Club and experience the joy of roller skating in a safe and fun environment. All ages are welcome, starting from 5 years old and upwards.

We provide all the necessary skating gear, including roller skates, so you can focus on having fun and staying active. Our sessions promote health, teamwork, and community bonding through themed nights and open community skates.

We also run regular afterschool roller skating sessions at:

  • Southern Road Primary School
  • Keir Hardie Primary School
  • Gascoigne Primary School

Our loyalty card scheme rewards participants for their dedication:

  • Children and Teens: Earn a free pair of roller skates after attending a certain number of sessions.
  • Adults: Receive a £30 gift voucher to put towards purchasing your own pair of skates.

Join us to roll into fun, fitness, and rewards!

Days and Time

  • Tuesdays: 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm
  • Wednesdays: 5:00 pm - 6:30 pm
  • Fridays: 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm

Smoothie Splash Bar

Our Smoothie Splash Bar is a mindfulness zone where youth can unwind, talk, and learn about healthy living. We host workshops teaching children, teens, and adults how to make delicious and nutritious smoothies for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

We also offer pop-up services at events, bringing the Smoothie Splash Bar experience to your community.

Touch of Urban

Our Young Urban Creators program teaches participants how to make eco-friendly jewelry using materials like wood, plastic, stones, and seashells. This program is open to individuals aged 8 and above.

Participants can also showcase and sell their creations at markets, with proceeds supporting our workshops and community activities.

Workshop Fee: £2 (learning how to make)

Workshop Time: Thursdays from 3:30 pm - 4:30 pm

Location: Barking Learning Centre, Youth Spot Bar Community Hub, IG11 7NB
